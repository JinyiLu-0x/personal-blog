I came across this line from Yubo while taking a break. Interesting, so I marked it:

> Frontend keeps getting stronger and is becoming the strongest.

Frontend is no longer only about “building the page.” Browsers, deployment, interaction, visualisation, Agent interfaces, and complete product prototypes increasingly begin directly from the frontend side.

That does not make backend, infrastructure, or engineering unimportant. It means frontend is becoming the place where users first feel many new tools.
