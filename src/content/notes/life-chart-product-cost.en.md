I left a very short thought when I saw this “life candlestick chart” product: when an opportunity appears, catching it is not only about noticing the idea. Development cost and execution ability matter too.

AI has reduced prototype costs a lot, but launching a real product still involves design, data, payments, content, security, and distribution. There is a long distance between seeing a good idea and building something usable that people will pay for.
