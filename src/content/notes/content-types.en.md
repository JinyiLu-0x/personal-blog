summary: 'Each content type has a different job: Blog explains the full process, Projects provides evidence, and Notes preserves lightweight context.'
comment: 'Separating content types is not about adding hierarchy; it gives each item an appropriate information density.'
visibility: 'public'
locale: 'en-US'

---

Blog supports complete arguments and project retrospectives. Projects should quickly answer what was built, what role I held, and what the outcome was. Notes preserves short observations, external links, and learning fragments that do not yet need expansion.

All three share tags without being forced into the same card or detail layout.
