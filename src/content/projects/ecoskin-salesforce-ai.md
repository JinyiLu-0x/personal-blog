## Background

EcoSkin is an enterprise retail system designed to connect customer inquiry, AI-assisted product recommendations, order placement, and post-sale support in one workflow.

## My Role

I designed the business object model, Salesforce automation, routing, and deployment setup, including the Digital Experiences site and embedded AI Agent.

## Implementation

- Built prompt- and workflow-driven recommendation and customer-support flows.
- Designed Omni-Channel routing so routine requests can be handled automatically and complex cases can be escalated.
- Developed Apex logic and automation for reminders, recommendations, order tracking, multi-channel communication, and Case management.
- Configured Embedded Service, Messaging Channel, CORS settings, and site deployment.

## Result

The project demonstrates an end-to-end Salesforce workflow rather than an isolated chatbot: customer interactions connect directly to orders, service operations, and escalation paths.
